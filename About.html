Japan holidays

Japan is one of the fastest growing destinations for travellers. The country is a spectacular mixture of tradition and the cosmopolition, where neon lights and packed skyscrapers meet hot springs, volcanic lakes and ancient temples.

With four main islands there are so many amazing places to visit, making it truly a destination with something for everyone! Adventurers will love the opportunites to hike, enjoy watersports and explore some of the countries fabulous ski resorts. Cultural lovers will adore Tokyo and Foodies will be in their element sampling one of the Worlds best cuisines.

Why Visit Japan?

  • The incredible natural landscapes including the iconic Mount Fuji
  • The Cherry Blossom season which is regarded as one of the most coveted natural sights
  • A country steeped in tradition, Japan boasts 21 World Heritage Sites. Explore majestic temples and shrines such as the Byodoin Buddhist temple in Uji which dates back 1000 years
  • The superb cuisine which is an incredibly important part of Japanese culture. The country also boasts the most 3-star Michelin restaurants!
  • Experience the future with the advanced and ultra-modern technology on display in all of the major cities
  • Kids and the young at heart can enjoy the magic of Disneyland Tokyo or animation lovers can visit the interactive Studio Ghibli museum which celebrates the films of one of Japan's most respected, Oscar-winning filmmakers
